Driving the Energy Revolution

More than an automaker — we are an energy & innovation company committed to accelerating the world’s transition to sustainable energy.

Our mission: Accelerate sustainable transport & energy

Tesla was founded in 2003 by a group of engineers who believed electric vehicles could be better, quicker, and more desirable than gasoline cars. Today, Tesla builds not only all-electric vehicles but also infinitely scalable clean energy generation and storage products. From Gigafactories to solar roofs, we are redefining what’s possible.
